Treatment

Fever

Medications such as paracetamol
and ibuprofen may help to ease discomfort. Avoid giving children aspirin because this may cause a rare, serious condition.

Common Cold

Rinsing the inside of the nose with warm salt water to flush out irritants and excess mucus.Often done using a neti pot or squeeze bottle
Soothes sore throats.
An oil made from mint that soothes
sore throats and relieves itching.

Cough

Liquids, lozenges, cough drops, vapourisers and steamy showers may help to soothe a cough. Cough medicine may also help, but it's best to check with a doctor before administering to a child under six
